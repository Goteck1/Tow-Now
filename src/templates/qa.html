Requesting a tow is simple! Go to our "Request Tow" page (easily accessible from the main menu or homepage). You will be guided through three steps:

  1. Enter Location: Provide your current location and where you need the vehicle towed. You can use GPS or type the address.
  2. Select Vehicle: Choose your vehicle type (e.g., car, SUV, motorcycle) to ensure we send the appropriate tow truck.
  3. Confirm & Pay: Review the estimated price and complete the secure online payment.
Once payment is confirmed, our system automatically assigns the nearest available tow truck to assist you promptly. You can also choose to "Continue as Guest" if you don\'t want to create an account immediately.

TowNow can handle a wide range of vehicles, including: Sedans, Hatchbacks, Compact Cars, SUVs, 4x4s, Small Vans, Large Vans, Pickup Trucks, Light Commercial Vehicles, Motorcycles, and Scooters. If you have a vehicle type not explicitly listed, you can select "Other" and provide details during the request process.

Our pricing is transparent. The cost is primarily based on the distance of the tow (from your current location to your specified destination) and the type of vehicle being towed. You will see a clear, estimated price before you confirm your request and make any payment. There are no hidden fees.
